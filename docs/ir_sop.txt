INCIDENT RESPONSE SOP
Version: 3.2
Department: Security Operations
Classification: Internal

Purpose:
This SOP defines the process for responding to cybersecurity incidents.

Severity Levels:
- Critical
- High
- Medium
- Low

Initial Triage Steps:
1. Validate alert authenticity.
2. Determine affected assets.
3. Identify indicators of compromise.
4. Escalate high-severity incidents to IR leadership.

Containment:
- Isolate compromised hosts.
- Disable affected accounts.
- Block malicious IP addresses.
- Preserve forensic evidence.

Eradication:
- Remove malware.
- Patch exploited vulnerabilities.
- Rotate credentials.

Recovery:
- Restore systems from backups.
- Validate system integrity.
- Monitor for reinfection.

Lessons Learned:
A post-incident review must occur within 72 hours.
